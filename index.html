Why does maple sap flow in spring?
Driven by freeze-thaw stem pressure — CO₂ dissolves at night creating tension, re-expands at day building up to 30 psi outward pressure...
My vacuum is lower than expected. How do I find the problem?
Systematic approach: isolate mainline sections one at a time, walk lines at dawn, inspect drop lines first — freeze-crack and UV brittleness cluster there...
What concentration should I target with my RO?
Most operations target 8–12°Brix for single-pass. Going above 16°Brix risks sucrose crystallization. UVM sensory studies show minimal flavor impact up to 16°Brix...
