Brix Trend + Buddy Sap Alert

Don't lose your
grade to bad timing.

Late-season sap looks identical to good sap — until you boil it. Buddy sap comes from trees that have begun to bud, and the syrup it makes is darker, cloudier, and carries an off-flavor that costs you your premium grade. You can't see it in the tank.

📉
SweetRun watches your Brix daily — log one reading per day and it plots the trend. A drop of more than 30% from your peak triggers a buddy sap warning.
🌡️
It cross-checks the 7-day forecast — if nights are holding above freezing, the season is over regardless of what the sap looks like in the tank.
🔔
You get the call early — boil what's in the tank today while you still have a run score worth acting on, and pull taps before buds blow.
